Assumptions


  • The calculator is in today's money - ie net of inflation
  • We assume that customer draws income from cash buffer that is replemished by selling down investments
  • We assume that the user will first draw down on other investments before drawing down pension funds
  • We assume that the user draws a pension commencement lump sum of 25% of the value of the pension on retirement, and reinvests this money into his/her ISA
  • If the user owns their property we assume that he/she draws on equity from this property if/when pension funds run out
